\section{Societal Considerations}
Table \ref{tab:cultural_group_parity} and Appendix Figure \ref{fig:cultural_parity} underscore the fairness implications of LLM cultural alignment, showing that models struggle to align with scenarios involving countries in the African Islamic or Orthodox Europe cultural groups. Similarly, we expect varying levels of cultural alignment across different demographics, communities, and perspectives, likely influenced by inherent biases in LLMs \cite{blodgett2020language, bender2021dangers, bai2024measuringimplicitbiasexplicitly}. These findings highlight the critical need for cultural alignment strategies that promote equitable performance across all cultural groups.
