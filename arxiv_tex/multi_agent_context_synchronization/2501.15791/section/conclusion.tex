\section{Conclusion}

In this paper, we propose MAKGED, a novel framework for knowledge graph error detection. 
% 
By combining subgraph embeddings from a GCN with LLM embeddings, we train four agents to evaluate triples through multi-agent discussions, enabling multi-perspective analysis. Experiments demonstrate that MAK-
GED significantly outperforms traditional and LLM-based methods, improving accuracy, F1-Score, precision, and recall across two datasets. Moreover, our framework has also shown excellent performance in industrial scenarios, validating the industrial application value of our method.
