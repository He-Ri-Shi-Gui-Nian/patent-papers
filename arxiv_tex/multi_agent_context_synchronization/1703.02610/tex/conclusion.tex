\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}
For modelling information gathering by a robot team, we presented $\rho$Dec-POMDP, extending the Dec-POMDP to information-theoretic rewards.
A $\rho$Dec-POMDPs may be solved applying existing algorithms for Dec-POMDPs, with modified reward function evaluation.
We verified the feasibility of our approach for cooperative target tracking.
Due to the adaptivity of $\rho$Dec-POMDP policies, they can outperform heuristic approaches.
Future work includes extended empirical evaluation, and possibly combining $\rho$Dec-POMDPs with distributed state estimation to relax communication assumptions.
