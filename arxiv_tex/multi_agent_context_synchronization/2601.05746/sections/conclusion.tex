\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}
We proposed \textbf{DynaDebate} to address reasoning homogeneity in multi-agent debate. By integrating dynamic path generation and allocation, process-centric debate, and trigger-based verification, our framework achieves superior or highly competitive performance across five benchmarks. We hope this work encourages future exploration of structured initialization heterogeneity and process-level verification in collaborative LLM reasoning systems.
