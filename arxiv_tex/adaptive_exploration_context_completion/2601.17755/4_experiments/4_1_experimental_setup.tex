
We evaluate our proposed methods on \textcolor{black}{three} multi-hop question answering benchmark datasets: 2WikiMultihopQA~(\textbf{2Wiki.})~\citep{ho2020constructing}, \textbf{HotPotQA}~\citep{yang2018hotpotqa}, \textbf{MuSiQue}~\citep{trivedi2022musique}.
We also adopt a large-scale single-hop QA dataset, Natural Questions (\textbf{NQ})~\citep{kwiatkowski2019natural} to explore the performance under the single-hop questions.
