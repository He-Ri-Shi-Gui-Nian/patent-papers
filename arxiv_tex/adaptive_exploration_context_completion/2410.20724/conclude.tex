\section{Conclusion}

We propose \proj, a KG-based RAG framework that performs efficient and flexible subgraph retrieval followed by prompting unfine-tuned LLMs. SubgraphRAG demonstrates better or comparable accuracy, efficiency, and explainability compared to existing approaches.
