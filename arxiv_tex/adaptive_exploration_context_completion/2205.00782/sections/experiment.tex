Notably, for GQE,
adding type information can shorten the gap or even surpass the state-of-the-art baseline models (without \textsc{Temp}).}

\paragraph{Deductive Reasoning.} \vic{Table \ref{table_1}(b) shows that after adding  type information, the reasoning ability of the baselines are significantly improved on all datasets consistently.
