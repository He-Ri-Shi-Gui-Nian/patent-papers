In this paper, we introduce GraphRAG-Router, a cost-efficient framework that coordinates GraphRAGs and generator LLMs through agentic routing. GraphRAG-Router leverages a hierarchical routing strategy and a two-stage RL training paradigm to balance performance and inference cost. Across six complex QA benchmarks, it outperforms all state-of-the-art baselines, achieving an average improvement of $22.3\%$ while reducing the overuse of large LLMs by nearly $30\%$.
